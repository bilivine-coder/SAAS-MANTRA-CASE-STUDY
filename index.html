Case Study — Automation ROI
How SaaS Mantra Fixed the "College Application" Onboarding & Unlocked $48k in Monthly Profit
The Challenge
The $3M Revenue Problem
SaaS Mantra had an impressive top line — bringing in $2.8M–$3.8M annually — but the bottom line was broken. Monthly profit fluctuated dangerously between $40k and $280k for that volume. The culprit was the onboarding form. It read like a college application. Long, confusing, and manual. 40–50% of new users dropped off before ever seeing the product dashboard — creating a cascade of low feature usage, a flood of support tickets, and hundreds of refund requests each year.
The Solution
From Manual Friction to Auto-Generated Flow
  • Onboarding Overhaul: Replaced the long-form application with a smart, progressive intake that auto-saves progress.
  • Automation Stack: Connected workflow for Auto-Generate Assets + Auto-Sync Data + Auto-Book Calls. If a user triggered "Need Help," the system booked the call — no human needed.
  • Smart Email Triage: Automated customer needs triage resolved common questions instantly, freeing the support team for real issues.
